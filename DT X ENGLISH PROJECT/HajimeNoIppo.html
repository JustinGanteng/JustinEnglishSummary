Hajime No Ippo

Ippo is a highschool student, his day is helping his mom send delivery for the fisherman,
his mom is the owner and operator of Makunouchi Fishing Boat. Ippo often get bullied by his seniors,
after school he get bullied under the bridge, then Takamura see's it, then Takamura help Ippo. Takamura
is a super middleweight boxer. Then Ippo was amazed to see how Takamura beat them, Ippo beg Takamura to teach him boxing,
at first Takamura doesn't want to teach Ippo boxing, Takamura gave Ippo a challenge to pick a leaves is the tree,
if Ippo can take at least 10, He will teach Ippo boxing. Ippo try so hard to get 10 leaves, he train all day.
At the day, Ippo prove that he can take 10 leaves in front of Takamura, and he succeed, so Takamura bring Ippo to his boxing gym.
At first, Genji, the main coach, he under estimated Ippo, so he ask Ippo to have a sparring against Miyata,
Ippo lose, but Genji was amazed of Ippo's stamina and endurance, so he accept Ippo to be his student. And Ippo's boxing career starts.
