Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a story about a boy name Itadori Yuji, He is a ordinary school boy, one day he meet Megumi Fushiguro,
a Jujutsu Sorcerer searching for a poweful cursed object known as Ryomen Sukuna finger. Yuji's friends accidentally unseal
that Cursed Object, attracting Curses to their location. Megumi and Itadori team up to fight the curses that came to their location.
In the middle of their fight, Yuji consumes Ryomen Sukuna finger and becames Sukuna host. After beating the curses and save
their friends, Yuji will be executed by the higher up socerer, but he managed to avoid the execution and became Satoru Gojo students,
the most powerful Jujutsu Sorcerer. Yuji meet other sorcerer like Megumi and Nobara Kugisaki, they became a team.
One day after Yuji have completed his training with Gojo, he was assigned to assist Kento Nanami to investigate the murders at Kinema Cinema.
That is where Itadori and Junpei met, Junpei is an eyewitness to the death of his classmate in the hand of Mahito, a villain.
In the meantime, Junpei and Mahito became closer. The investigation continues further, Nanami decided to find Mahito hideout,
and a battle between Nanami and Mahito just started. Mahito tells Junpei to kill one of his classmate. Before Junpei can act,
Itadori came to stop Junpei, leading to a fight between Itadori and Mahito. Itadori almost able to kill Mahito,
but Mahito escapes at the last moment. However, the burden of not able to save Junpei killing Itadori inside.
The Kyoto Kyoto Goodwill event finally started, Kyoto team receives orders to kill Yuji from Gokuganji,
while Aoi refuses to take part in their plan. The friendly match has started, with Aoi confronting Yuji.
Yuji's moves impress Aoi, and they start building a strong connection. Meanwhile the other Kyoto team try to takedown Yuji, Aoi stands against his own mates.
The appearance of Hanami, a formidable curse, disrupts the event. This becomes an even more opportunity for Aoi and Yuji to strengthen their bond.
In the end Hanami is killed by Gojo. Yuji and his friends are assigned to a mission, to fight a death paintings. Megumi fights against Finger Bearer,
and despite the Curse High potential, Megumi defeated it. In the meantime, Nobara and Yuji encounter the Death Painting, Eso and Kechizu, and the former take Nobara hostage.
With combined effort, they finally beat them. Gojo is impressed by his students and promoted them for an upper rank.
